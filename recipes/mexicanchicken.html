Description

Amazingly easy recipe and makes a delicious quesadilla when served in a toasted tortilla.

Ingredients

  • 3 Chicken Breasts
  • White onion, chopped small
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  • Can chopped tomatoes
  • Tsp chipotle paste

Steps

  1. Fry the onion and garlic until soft
  2. Put all Ingredients into slow cooker
  3. Cook on high for approx 3 hours
  4. Shred the chicken with a fork
  5. Serve inside a toasted tortilla with soured cream and guac on the top. I just toast mine in a frying pan.
