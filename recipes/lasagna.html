Description

This is J's best lasagna recipe. Tried and tested over many moons.

Best served with garlic bread and salad

Ingredients

Tomato Sauce

  • 250g beef mince
  • 250g pork mince
  • Carrot - chopped small
  • 2 sticks celery chopped small
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 2 tins tomatoes
  • Chicken stock cube
  • herbs
  • Tomato Puree

Bechemel Sauce

  • 600 mls milk
  • 60g butter
  • 60g flour
  • Tsp dijon mustard
  • Pinch nutmeg
  • Bay leaf

Other

  • Lasagna Sheets

Steps

  1. Make tomato sauce
  2. Make Bechemel sauce
  3. Mix and leave small amount of Bechemel to the side
  4. Layer the sauces and the pasta sheets in a lasagna dish
  5. Finish with a layer of bechemel and some basil leaves for decoration
  6. Cover with foild and cook for 45 mins
  7. Remove foil and cook for final 10 mins
  8. Serve and enjoy
